12 min

"Lie Until Real" Method
The 5 programs were installed exactly this way: a kid "lied" to himself → brain found proof → belief solidified. Same mechanism works in reverse.

1. Install deliberately. Theta, breathwork, scripts.
2. Act as if. Fill every hour building, training, creating.
3. Weaponize confirmation bias. Notice contentment alone. Log it.
4. Cognitive dissonance. Behavior contradicts old belief → brain updates BELIEF.
5. Evidence accumulates. The "lie" stops being a lie.

Timeline: 21-30 days consistent. After 60, it's default.
