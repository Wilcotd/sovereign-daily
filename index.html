"Lie Until Real" Method
The 5 programs were installed exactly this way: a kid "lied" to himself that he needed social proof → brain found proof → belief solidified. Same mechanism works in reverse.

The belief: "I am complete on my own. A girl is a bonus, not a need."

1. Install deliberately. Theta every night. Breathwork holds. Third-person script.
2. Act as if. Stop seeking. Fill every hour with building, training, creating.
3. Weaponize confirmation bias. Notice moments of genuine contentment alone. Log them.
4. Cognitive dissonance. When behavior (acting complete) contradicts old belief → brain updates the BELIEF.
5. Evidence accumulates. The "lie" stops being a lie.

Timeline: 21-30 days consistent. After 30 days, new belief wins most retrieval competitions. After 60, it's default.
