## Best Areas for Value

- **Lamai Beach** - Cheaper than Chaweng, good beach, lots of options
- **Bophut / Fisherman Village** - Local markets + relaxed seaside
- **Maenam** - Tranquil beaches, budget-friendly
- **West Coast** - Quieter, great sunsets, lower prices
